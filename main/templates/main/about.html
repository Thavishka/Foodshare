About FoodShare

Connecting surplus food with communities in need

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and fight hunger by creating a seamless connection between food donors and receivers, ensuring that surplus food reaches those who need it most while promoting environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

A world where no edible food goes to waste and every community has access to nutritious meals, creating sustainable and food-secure communities for future generations.

The Problem We Solve

Food waste is a global crisis with local solutions.

How FoodShare Works

1. Donate Food

Restaurants, catering services, events, and households can easily list their surplus food with details about type, quantity, allergens, and pickup location.

  • Simple listing process
  • Food safety guidelines
  • Flexible pickup times

2. Connect & Request

Registered NGOs, charities, shelters, and individuals in need can browse available food donations and submit requests based on their requirements.

  • Real-time availability
  • Location-based matching
  • Secure communication

3. Collect & Distribute

Receivers collect the food directly from donors at scheduled times, ensuring fresh food reaches those who need it while reducing environmental impact.

  • Scheduled pickups
  • Quality assurance
  • Impact tracking

Our Values

Sustainability

We believe in creating sustainable solutions to food waste that benefit both the environment and communities. Every meal saved reduces greenhouse gas emissions and conserves resources.

Compassion

Building strong community connections is at the heart of everything we do. We treat every donor, receiver, and volunteer with respect and empathy.

Safety First

Food safety is our top priority. We ensure all donations meet safety standards and provide clear guidelines for proper food handling and storage.

Collaboration

We work together with donors, receivers, and community partners to maximize our impact. Together, we can achieve what none of us can do alone.
