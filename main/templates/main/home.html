How It Works

1. Post Available Food

Restaurants, events, or households can list surplus food with details like type, quantity, and pickup location.

2. NGOs & Individuals Request

Registered NGOs or individuals can browse and request available food donations.

3. Connect & Collect

Once approved, receivers can collect the food, reducing waste and helping communities.
